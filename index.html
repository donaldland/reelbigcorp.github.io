Our Vision

Founded in 2024, Reel Big Corporation emerged from a passion for both classic filmmaking techniques and modern digital innovation. We believe in combining the nostalgic charm of retro visual aesthetics with contemporary production methods to create unique, compelling content. We're proud to be a partner with Reel Big Studios.

Our Approach

At Reel Big Corporation, we embrace the imperfections and warmth of analog equipment while leveraging the precision and flexibility of digital technologies. This fusion allows us to craft visuals that feel both familiar and fresh, resonating with audiences across generations.

Our Team

Our diverse team consists of filmmakers, editors, and visual artists who share a deep appreciation for the history of cinema and a keen eye for modern visual trends. Each member brings unique expertise, whether it's mastering vintage equipment or pushing the boundaries of digital effects.

Our Journey

2018

Reel Big Studios Established

Founding of Reel Big Studios, setting the groundwork for our creative vision in film production.

2019-2024

Feature Film Production

Produced three feature films: "Knights of Swing," "Tomorrow," and "Forty Seven Days with Jesus."

2021-2023

Nike Campaign

Completed a two-year long Nike campaign for "Selected By," expanding our commercial portfolio.

2024

Reel Big Corporation Founded

Establishment of Reel Big Corporation, merging retro aesthetics with modern filmmaking techniques.
